Tigrinya Dictionary

Tigrinya Dictionary is a complete offline reference application designed to empower language learners with instant access to over 50,000 words and definitions without internet connectivity.

The app features bidirectional search between English and Tigrinya, an integrated keyboard, personalized learning tools, and support for 12 languages, making it the perfect companion for travelers and language enthusiasts.

Key Features

100% Offline Access

Access over 50,000 words and definitions without internet connection. Perfect for travel or areas with limited connectivity.

Bidirectional Search

Seamlessly search words in both English and Tigrinya Dictionary with instant results and comprehensive definitions.

Integrated Keyboard

Built-in Tigrinya keyboard makes typing effortless, eliminating the need for additional keyboard installations.

Personalization Tools

Save custom words with Notes, track Recent Searches, and create your personal Favorites list for quick access to frequently used terms.

Design Process

UI Improvements & Updates

Before Update

Initial design featuring a darker blue theme with prominent action buttons and simplified word suggestions at the bottom of the screen.

After Update

Refined design with a lighter color scheme, improved readability, streamlined action buttons, and enhanced "You Might Also Like" recommendations for better word discovery.

App Performance & User Engagement

The app has achieved remarkable success with 38,500 total downloads, a 4.6-star rating from 1,360 reviews, and 1.81 million impressions. With a 2.5% conversion rate and strong presence in key markets including the US, UK, Canada, and China.

Design Philosophy

Our design philosophy centers on accessibility, personalization, and seamless offline functionality. Every feature is crafted to provide instant value without barriers, ensuring users can learn effectively regardless of their location or internet connectivity.
