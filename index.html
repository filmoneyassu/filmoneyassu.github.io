About Me

Hi, I'm Filmon!

I'm a UX/UI Designer, iOS Developer, and Backend Developer based in Bergen, Norway. I create meaningful digital experiences by combining design skills with practical development expertise.

I hold a UX design degree from Noroff School of Technology and Digital Media, with training in UX research, inclusive design, wireframes, and prototyping using Figma and Adobe XD.

I'm a self-taught iOS developer with 6 years of experience and am currently studying backend development. This combination allows me to create user-centered solutions from design to implementation.
