About Me

Hi, I'm Filmon!

I'm a UX/UI Designer, iOS Developer, and Backend Developer from Bergen, Norway. I create useful digital experiences by blending design and development.

I have a UX design degree from Noroff School of Technology and Digital Media, where I trained in UX research, inclusive design, wireframing, and prototyping with Figma and Adobe XD.

I'm a self-taught iOS developer with a few years of experience, and I'm currently learning backend development. This mix helps me create user-focused solutions from start to finish.
